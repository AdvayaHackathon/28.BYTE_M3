Arunachal Pradesh, Northeast

Ziro Valley

A scenic valley home to the Apatani tribe and known for its rice cultivation and pine-clad hills. The annual Ziro Music Festival brings artists from around the world.

Assam, Northeast

Majuli Island

The world's largest river island, Majuli is a cultural hub of Assamese civilization with unique monasteries called Satras. Experience mask-making traditions and serene riverine landscapes.
