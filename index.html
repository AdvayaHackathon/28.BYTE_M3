About Us

YatraGPT was founded in 2025 with a vision to revolutionize cultural tourism through technology. Our team of passionate travelers, technologists, and cultural experts came together to create a platform that bridges the gap between traditional tourism and modern technology.

We believe that every journey should be more than just a vacation - it should be an opportunity to connect with different cultures, understand their heritage, and contribute positively to the communities we visit.

Today, YatraGPT serves travelers worldwide, helping them discover authentic cultural experiences while promoting sustainable tourism practices.

×

Our Mission

Our mission is to transform cultural tourism by:

  • Making cultural experiences accessible to everyone through technology
  • Promoting sustainable and responsible tourism practices
  • Supporting local communities and preserving cultural heritage
  • Fostering meaningful connections between travelers and local cultures
  • Using AI to create personalized cultural experiences

We strive to be the leading platform that connects travelers with authentic cultural experiences while ensuring the preservation and celebration of global heritage.
